Assignment 8: Learning Figma

Alrighty, this assignment was quite the breath of fresh air! My brain enjoyed a nice vacation from brackets, at least for the week! Figma was pretty easy to get the hang of, and I got to make a poster design which is honestly one of my favorite things to do! Figma is really practical for planning formatting since it gives you options of different devices to test out your compositions. This really helped me prepare for the next assignment by providing me with some guidelines to follow so I wouldn't feel totally lost converting this content into html.
